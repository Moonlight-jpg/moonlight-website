About Us


Moonlight Breeze is a mobile dry-hire bartending service dedicated to creating unforgettable experiences for you and your guests. Whether it's craft beers, fine wines, or expertly mixed cocktails, we serve a comprehensive range of beverages to suit every occasion.

We also specialize in customizing creative cocktail names, adding a personalized and fun touch to your event. From weddings and parties to corporate events and beyond, Moonlight Breeze is committed to tailoring each event to your specific vision, ensuring a refined, exceptional experience that exceeds expectations.

We look forward to talking with you!

Meet The Owner


Moonlight Breeze is a professional and reputable business owned and operated by Annie Lou, a certified mixologist with expertise in crafting exceptional beverages. Annie Lou is also a holder of a ServSafe certification, ensuring the highest standards of safety and hygiene in food and beverage service.

Moonlight Breeze is a fully insured Limited Liability Company (LLC), demonstrating its commitment to operating with the highest level of professionalism and protection.
